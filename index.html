Starting Line

Inspiring women to follow their passion for cars, empowering the new generation of girls to find their confidence in the drivers seat.

This sport can be seen as a "man's" world. We are setting out to show women can have a love for automobiles. They can appercatite the bodylines of a clasic, and the power of an engine.

We love to highlight the women who show cars, and the racers of the sport. Proving that it is OKAY to have grease under that manicure.

Finish Line

Providing the womans touch to the world of motorsports. We work with young girls, to women of all ages in providing them a place to explore their passion for cars.
